# SafeCore Enterprise: User Guide

## Getting Started
Welcome to the SafeCore Warehouse Safety Platform. This tool helps you manage Standard operating Procedures (SOPs), Job Safety Analyses (JSAs), and Work Instructions.

## Navigation
- **Dashboard**: High-level overview of site safety KPIs and your pending tasks.
- **Document Library**: The central repository for all approved safety protocols.
- **Corrective Actions**: Track safety improvements and audit findings.
- **Training**: Complete your assigned safety training modules.

## Creating Protocols
1. Navigate to **Document Library** -> **+ Deploy New Protocol**.
2. Fill in the required metadata (Site, Department, Document Type).
3. Use the **AI Safety Assistant** to help draft steps or identify hazards.
4. Submit for Review to notify authorized reviewers.

## Approvals & Publishing
- Documents must pass a multi-stage approval workflow before being "Published".
- Only Published documents are considered "Effective" for site operations.

## Mobile Use
SafeCore is responsive. Use it on tablets for floor audits or mobile phones for quick training checks. Touch targets are optimized for industrial environments.
